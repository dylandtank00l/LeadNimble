FAQ

What’s covered in the 10-hour max Quick Start?

Typical wins: lead routing fixes, data hygiene, small automations, small data projects, and reporting cleanup. Bigger items move to a scoped project.

Can we roll remaining time into a retainer?

Yes — any unused time can be credited toward ongoing admin support if started within 30 days.
